Genomely combines genome evolution and software engineering to elucidate the origin of phenotypes important to human welfare, democratize bioinformatics, and pave the way for data sharing and accessibility.

The group develops free, easy-to-use tools such as ClipKIT, an alignment trimming toolkit, and highlights recent innovations through the Genomely blog. Featured articles and announcements are shared below.
